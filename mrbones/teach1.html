origins

It is time that I put this great tale into the immortilization of text. The following is what we like to call Chapter 0 of the Dankizn Chronicles.

What is Dankizn? That is the question that many new, young disciples ask me. To answer that question, though, we first much journey into the very origins of this world.

In the beginning, it is said that there was nothing.

The universe as we know it simply did not exist.

In a way unfathomable to us who are limited to existence within our own vessels, everything was nothing.

That was, until Jesus Kang.

Through means we will likely never be able to understand, Jesus Kang was able to exist in nothing.

And, for reason not understood, Jesus Kang would then go on to create the universe as we know it

He did this monumental act, simply by expanding his dong.

His dong, the first true example of matter to exist in the nothingness of the primordial universe, became the universe.

He looked into the universe from outside it, and he understood everything.

But, he could not truly do anything if he was to simply remain outside of the universe.

So, he transfered his mind into an immortal body that he created inside of his dong, and entered the universe.

For a while, he roamed, exploring his creations.

Space, stars, galaxies, they are all beautiful to us.

But to Jesus Kang, who was before all, and had seen everything without the lenses of a mortal body.

Beauty, held no meaning, nor did fascination.

He was beggining to become bored, as he had no stimuli to keep him entertained at all.

So, he created life on the rocky planet we now know as Earth.

He split his soul into many, many, pieces, and made a pool out of it

Each new living being took part of his soul from the pool, and could, in their mortal body, experience the universe.

In essence, allowing Jesus Kang himself to experience.

Eventually, this life became very complex, and displayed evidence of intelligence

He deicded to bless his favorite life, specifically, the ancestors of the gorillas.

But this blessing did not last long, for the creatures stole his supply of bananas-- the holy food that contains the energy that powers the very soul.

Instead, Jesus Kang then favoured a different, similar species, humans.

He watched over them, and provided them great power

He granted them access to the holy energies: Slam Energy, Banana Energy, Dank Power, and, most powerful of all, Jam Essence.

With these powers, humans quickly rose to the top of Earth.

They became more advanced, too, eventually forming civilizations.

To help him watch over the humans, he decided to create a son.

This son, would be named Dankey Kang.

After this, there was a long period of harmony between Jesus Kang, Dankey Kang, and the humans.

The two kangs and the humans even mingled a bit.

Through the wombs of several human women, Dankey Kang would have 23 sons and daughters, who would become known as the Kongs.

The Kongs had powers similar to a Kang, but were not nearly as powerful.

Jesus Kang would do the same, having seven children of his own.

The seven children would become known as the Lower Kangs

Their power has near the level of Dankey Kang.

With this, the Kangs and Kongs were born, and the humans, with their support, were growing every day.

Things would carry on in this manner for a while, until eventually, tragedy struck.

But that, is a story for next time.
